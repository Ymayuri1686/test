E-Governance Mindmap

What is E-Governance?

E-Governance uses ICT to provide services and enable citizen, business, and government transactions efficiently and transparently.

Applications of E-Governance

Digital Public Services

G2C - Government to Citizen

G2B - Government to Business

G2G - Government to Government

G2N - Government to NGO

Impact of E-Governance

Makes governance faster, transparent, accountable, and inclusive for citizens and government.

Usage of E-Governance

Citizen services, business facilitation, internal operations, healthcare, and education services delivery.
